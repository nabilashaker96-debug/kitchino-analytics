Forecast Methodology
Uses Trend + Seasonality decomposition. Historical monthly data is split into a linear growth trend and a calendar-month seasonal pattern. Future values are projected as trend × seasonal index. The shaded confidence band represents ±1.5 standard deviations of historical residuals. Accuracy decreases beyond 3 months.
